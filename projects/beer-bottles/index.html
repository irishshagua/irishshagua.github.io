My Beer Bottle Collection

On dislay in my house is the fruit of several years of foraging. Beer bottles from around the world. Each drank by myself and each with their own story. See the collection below.

Name Brand Style Description
Taedonggang Taedonggang Brewing Company Lager This was the most difficult bottle to get by far. It's from a brewery in North Korea and it needed to be shipped to Ireland via a hop in China (the only country the brewery deals with). A pretty normal tasting lager at the end of it but the historic charm is there for all to see
More Info
Fallout Beer Carlsberg Lager A fairly standard beer but the bottle is from a survival game that I loved as a kid. If you haven't played Fallout, you really should check it out.
